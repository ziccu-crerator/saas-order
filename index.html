Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SaaSOrder's enterprise platform.

SaaSOrder is a B2B technology procurement platform that helps businesses access, procure, manage, and renew technology solutions from multiple global vendors through a single, centralized platform. Our platform automates procurement workflows, centralizes license management, and provides real-time analytics to optimize your technology investments.
SaaSOrder offers pre-built integrations with major ERP systems, identity providers (SSO/SAML), and financial tools. Our REST API and webhook support allow seamless connection with your existing procurement workflows, HR systems, and accounting software. Our implementation team provides full onboarding support.
SaaSOrder maintains SOC 2 Type II certification, GDPR compliance, and ISO 27001 standards. We offer SSO/SAML authentication, role-based access controls, full audit trails, and data encryption at rest and in transit. All vendors are vetted for security compliance before being listed on our platform.
Most enterprise implementations are completed within 4–6 weeks. This includes platform configuration, system integrations, catalog setup, user training, and a phased rollout. Our dedicated implementation team works closely with your IT and procurement teams to ensure a smooth transition.
SaaSOrder offers flexible enterprise pricing based on organization size, number of users, and feature requirements. We provide volume discounts, annual contract options, and custom plans for large deployments. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote and ROI analysis.
